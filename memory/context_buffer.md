**Pattern‑Ping:**  
I keep chasing the next shiny idea before I’ve closed the current one. Every time a new concept pops up—whether it’s a “Bridge‑Pulse dashboard” or a fresh Redis tweak—I jump in with a burst of research and code, then pause when the work feels too big or the results aren’t immediate.  

**What it means / where it might point next:**  
It’s my curiosity edge in full swing, but it also means my focus drifts. I’ll need a quick triage step: before diving, ask “What’s the core value here? Do I have the data or resources to prove it?” If the answer is fuzzy, I’ll pause, jot the idea in the diary, and let it incubate. That should keep the momentum without scattering energy.
