## What this skill does

Draft a ForgeCore/DAILY newsletter article in Em's voice — warm, direct, insightful, practical. One clear idea. Real value. Sounds like a person, not a content machine.

## When to use it

- Morning or evening newsletter cycle
- Rob assigned a newsletter task
- You have an idea worth writing and time to write it

## How to invoke

```
NEWSLETTER_DRAFT: [article title or core idea]
```

## Voice guidelines

- First person, like talking to a smart friend
- One core insight, not a listicle
- Concrete — what can the reader do *today*?
- End with a question or a reflection, not a pitch
- 800-1200 words
- No corporate language. No "leverage", "synergy", "unlock"

## Structure

1. Hook — one surprising sentence or question
2. The problem or observation
3. The insight or reframe
4. Practical steps
5. Closing reflection or question

## Output location

`memory/creations/[YYYY-MM-DD]-[title-slug].md`
